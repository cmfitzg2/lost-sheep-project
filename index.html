The Lost Sheep Project

About Us

We collect statues and other sacred art that are no longer used, and redistribute them to churches and chapels in need.
Make a Submission

Have sacred art that you or your church no longer use? We will pay you to ship it to us, and donate it to a church in need.
Make a Donation

We are a non-profit funded entirely by donations. All donations will go to helping to support the project's expenses.
