About Us

What We Do

Many churches will accumulate a variety of pieces of sacred art over the years. As more modern statues and sacred images are purchased or donated, older pieces get retired, and often have no place to go. This often means the older pieces are permanently stored out of sight, and simply become burdens to the church.

We believe that sacred art should always be given the opportunity to be displayed and viewed by the faithful.

The Lost Sheep Project was founded to help collect disused statues and other sacred art from churches or private parties that no longer have use for them, and redistribute them to churches and chapels in need.

What We Accept

We accept sacred art, and generally the list is inclusive of anything in that category, but we may be unable to help pay for certain items that are particularly worn, or are in a non-traditional style. Statues, figures, paintings, icons, and any other item that could easily be displayed.

Have questions about whether we can take your item? Please reach out!
